Multiple spectra¶

Resistics supports the calculation of multiple spectra for each data folder. This is achieved through the specdir option.

exampleProject
├── calData
├── timeData
│   └── site1
|       |── dataFolder1
│       |── dataFolder2
|       |──     .
|       |──     .
|       |──     .
|       └── dataFolderN
├── specData
│   └── site1
|       |── dataFolder1
|       |   |── newSpectra
|       |   └── spectra
|       |
│       |── dataFolder2
|       |   |── newSpectra
|       |   └── spectra
|       |──     .
|       |──     .
|       |──     .
|       └── dataFolderN
|           |── newSpectra
|           └── spectra
├── statData
├── maskData
├── transFuncData
├── images
└── mtProj.prj
